Privacy Policy

ADHD AI TRACKER  ·  Last updated: March 2, 2026

ADHD AI TRACKER is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

Information We Collect

Habit Data. The app stores your habits, completion logs, achievements, XP, and streaks locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers or any external parties.

Usage Analytics. We may collect anonymous usage data to help improve the app experience. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Premium Subscriptions

All subscription payments are processed through Apple's In-App Purchase system. We do not collect, store, or process any payment information. All transactions are handled securely by Apple.

Information We Do Not Collect

ADHD AI TRACKER does not require account registration. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information. No data is transmitted to external servers. All habit data, progress, and achievements are stored exclusively on your device.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not share any data with third parties.

Children Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by deleting the app from your device.

Contact

Questions? Contact us through the App Store.
