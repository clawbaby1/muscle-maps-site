Muscle Maps Support

Real Activity Game

Thanks for playing Muscle Maps! If you are running into an issue or have a question, we are here to help.

The radar map is not showing my location

Make sure location access is enabled for Muscle Maps. Go to Settings > Privacy and Security > Location Services > Muscle Maps and set it to "While Using the App".

Steps are not being counted

The app uses your iPhone motion sensor. Go to Settings > Privacy and Security > Motion and Fitness and make sure Muscle Maps is enabled. If you also want HealthKit step syncing, go to Settings > Health > Data Access and Devices > Muscle Maps and enable step count access.

The app is crashing on launch

Try closing the app fully and reopening it. If the problem persists, delete and reinstall the app from the App Store. Your progress is stored locally, so a reinstall will reset game data.
